Revitalizing the Brand: Crafting a Clear Vision and Designing a Captivating Identity on a conservative market

KEY HEALTHCARE

Design — Brand Strategy — Production — Brand platform — Identity

1/19

Working with the task of building a brand, we as an agency had to face various previous reincarnations of identities. The client, a healthcare company in CA, USA, existed in a constantly changing inner world of its visual looks. We worked with an interesting situation in which we had to connect new identity with previous.

Finding individuality was another important aspect of our work. We helped analyze the brand's unique qualities and put them into words, ensuring that physical and digital touchpoints were implemented to create consistent and meaningful brand interactions.

We also considered the needs of Generation Z, as they were the primary target audience in the mental health field. Personalization and real connection became crucial elements in our design, as these are the values that resonate with Generation Z. By understanding their preferences and incorporating these elements into the brand strategy, we were able to create a brand identity that appealed to this specific demographic.

A brand that not only fit into a conservative market but also stood out as a dynamic and innovative force in the healthcare industry.

Childlike Creativity: Journey to Memorable Family Experiences

ISKRA FAMILY GROUP

Design — Key Visual — Creative concept

ISKRA FAMILY GROUP is a beloved brand in the eco-tourism field. Client’s team has developed a clear strategy and a deep understanding of their target audience.

As a creative agency, In our work on brand identity, we have crafted a visual code that embodies the value of childlike drawings as a symbol of creativity that flourishes within families. Our series of illustrations seamlessly blend with other content, marking it and instantly setting it apart in the minds of the audience both parents and kids through recognizable imagery. However, it was done so delicately and in an environmentally friendly manner. Client successfully and easily implement new branding on merchandise which resonates with travellers who wear it with pleasure and it serves as a reminder of their meaningful and enjoyable family experiences.
